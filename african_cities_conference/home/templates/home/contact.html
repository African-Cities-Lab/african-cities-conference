{% blocktranslate %} The first annual meeting of the academic and professional world on African cities, will highlight the solutions brought by urban actors to create new city models capable of accommodating the 950 million additional inhabitants who will populate urban environments by 2050. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate %} The first edition of this international event will present digital and smart cities as sustainable, inclusive and innovative solutions to the urban challenges facing Africa. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% translate "Novotel Cotonou Orisha" %}

{% blocktranslate %} The welcome of the participants to the 1st edition of the African Cities Conference will be done at the Novotel hotel. {% translate "Hosting" %}

{% blocktranslate %} A part of the rooms of the Novotel Hotel in Cotonou has been reserved for the African Cities Conference. Other 3 and 4 star hotels will be announced soon and reservation details will be communicated to participants as soon as possible. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% translate "Visa" %}

{% blocktranslate %} Nationals of countries subject to the visa application procedure can now apply for a visa online (e-visa) at https://evisa.bj/. It takes 7 days to obtain a visa. If necessary, the organisers will send you an official letter of participation, justifying your travel from your country of origin to Benin. {% endblocktranslate %}
