229 Cadre de Vie
African Design School

{% blocktranslate %} A partnership has been set up between Africa Design School and the Ministry of the Living Environment and Sustainable Development in order to implement a solution to the problems identified. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate %} As part of its mission to improve the living environment, the Ministry wishes to develop a digital solution that allows people to collaborate with its teams to help improve their living environment. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate %} « 229 Cadre de Vie » is a service that will enable citizens to play a role in improving their living environment. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate %} Les utilisateurs pourront interagir avec l’équipe du ministère en signalant les dysfonctionnements constatés dans la rue, que ce soit des problématiques de propreté, de voirie ou de circulation. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate %} With a few clicks and a photo, users can become active citizens and participate in improving our living environment. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate %} The report will be processed by the relevant technical department and users will be able to track the progress of their requests. {% endblocktranslate %}
