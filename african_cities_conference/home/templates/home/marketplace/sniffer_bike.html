{% translate "Sniffer Bike: Measure air pollution in real time while cycling!" %}

{% blocktranslate %} Have you ever wanted to participate in the collection of air quality data in your city? If so, we have a solution for you: Sniffer Bike! {% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate %} The Sniffer Bike solution consists of air pollution sensors embedded in shared bikes. These gas and particulate matter (PM) sensors use low-cost technology to provide real-time air quality data from the bikes' location. air quality data in real time from the location of the bikes. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate %} With Sniffer Bike, we can enjoy the convenience of cycling (can be shared) or other public transport while contributing to the collection of air quality data. public transport while contributing to the collection of air quality data. The data is stored in a spatial database The data is stored in a spatial database, which allows the creation of interactive maps of the air quality in your city. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate %} The first Sniffer Bike prototype is currently in the test phase as a handheld device. Our goal is to generalise this solution in shared bikes in the near future, so that everyone can contribute to the collection of air quality data in their city. can contribute to the collection of air quality data in their city. With your participation, we can make this solution a reality for everyone. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate %} Our Sniffer Bike solution does not just collect air quality data. It goes further by developing decision support tools for decision makers. These decision aids provide decision makers with valuable information on air quality These decision aids provide decision-makers with valuable information on air quality, which can help them to take appropriate action in terms of environmental protection. With Éco-Vélo, we can contribute to the creation of a cleaner and healthier environment for all, while helping decision makers to make informed decisions. {% endblocktranslate %}
